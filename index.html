The Story

Scene 01

stranger.

Scene 02

Where the road
meets the river.

The Baspa walks beside the bus. Someone opens a window. Cold mountain air rushes in. Nobody complains.

Scene 03

Welcome to

Green disappears. The earth turns the colour of an old film print. Above us, a sky so close you could reach up and pull it down.

Scene 04

Candles inside a meditation cave. Monks who never wanted to be remembered. Somehow you feel them anyway.

Scene 05
